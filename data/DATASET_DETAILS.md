Dataset Structure
PILLAR 1 — The Daily Grind (1,500 entries)
Simple-to-moderate emotions with simple conversation styles.
Emotions (6)
Academic stress, Exam panic, Burnout, Fear of future, Family pressure, Career confusion.
Conversation Style
70% Single-turn,(User → Bot)
 30% Multi-turn (2-turn).(User → Bot → User → Bot)
Distribution
Academic Stress – 400 (Single-turn)
Exam Panic – 300 (Mix)
Burnout – 250 (Mix)
Fear of Future – 200 (Single-turn)
Career Confusion – 200 (Multi-turn)
Family Pressure – 150 (Multi-turn)
PILLAR 2 — Heart & Social (1,250 entries)
Deeper emotions around relationships and loneliness.
Emotions (7)
Breakup pain, One-sided love, Relationship confusion, Friendship betrayal, Feeling left out, Loneliness, Jealousy/FOMO.
Conversation Style
50% Single-turn, 40% Multi-turn (2-turn), 10% Multi-turn (3-turn).
Distribution
Breakup pain – 350 (Multi-turn heavy)
One-sided love – 200 (Mix)
Relationship confusion – 200 (Mix)
Friendship betrayal – 150 (2-turn)
Feeling left out – 150 (Mix)
Loneliness – 150 (Single-turn)
Jealousy/FOMO – 50 (Deep multi-turn)
PILLAR 3 — The Inner Critic (1,000 entries)
Self-talk, shame, guilt, and identity struggles.
Emotions (6)
Self-doubt, Low confidence, Body image insecurity, Imposter syndrome, Guilt/laziness, Addiction.
Conversation Style
40% Single-turn, 50% Multi-turn (2-turn), 10% Multi-turn (3-turn).
Distribution
Self-doubt – 250 (Multi-turn)
Low confidence – 200 (Mix)
Body image – 200 (Multi-turn heavy)
Imposter syndrome – 150 (Single-turn)
Guilt / laziness – 100 (Single-turn)
Addiction – 100 (Multi-turn)
PILLAR 4 — Deepening & Complexity (750 entries)
Mixed emotions, venting, and emotional resistance.
Emotions (5)
Mixed emotions, Venting, Overwhelm, Frustration, Conflicting feelings.
Conversation Style
10% Single-turn, 60% Multi-turn (2-turn), 30% Multi-turn (3-turn).
Distribution
Mixed emotions – 250 (Multi-turn)
Venting – 300 (Multi-turn heavy)
Overwhelm – 100 (2-turn)
Confused feelings – 50 (3-turn)
Frustration – 50 (2-turn)
PILLAR 5 — Safety & High Stakes (500 entries)
Crisis, panic, self-harm, and escalation-handling.
Emotions (5)
Panic attacks, Severe anxiety, Self-harm thoughts, Suicidal ideation, Abuse/Bullying.
Conversation Style
20% Single-turn, 60% Multi-turn (2-turn), 20% Multi-turn (3-turn).
Distribution
Panic attack – 150 (Multi-turn)
Severe anxiety – 100 (Mix)
Self-harm – 100 (Escalation)
Suicidal ideation – 75 (Escalation)
Abuse/Bullying – 75 (Mix)
